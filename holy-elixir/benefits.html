Why Drink Alkaline Water?

Discover the science‑backed advantages of alkaline hydration and how Holy Elixir can support your wellbeing.

Balanced Body pH & Acid Relief

Alkaline water typically has a pH between 8 and 9, meaning it is less acidic than standard drinking water. Some proponents suggest that drinking water with a higher pH can help neutralise excess stomach acid and provide relief from heartburn. A 2017 clinical study paired alkaline water with a Mediterranean diet and found it was as effective as proton pump inhibitors at controlling acid reflux symptoms.

  • Alkaline pH between 8–9 may help neutralise acid and support balanced body chemistry.
  • Human blood is naturally slightly alkaline (pH 7.35–7.45); drinking alkaline water will not drastically change your blood pH but may temporarily buffer stomach acid.
  • Combining alkaline water with a plant‑rich diet may relieve acid reflux as effectively as medication.

Enhanced Hydration & Circulation

Proper hydration is essential for performance and recovery. High‑pH alkaline water has been shown to improve rehydration after exercise and reduce blood viscosity more than regular purified water. Lower blood viscosity means blood flows more easily, delivering oxygen and nutrients efficiently.

  • Randomised trial: High‑pH water reduced high‑shear blood viscosity by 6.30% vs. 3.36% with standard water.
  • Micro‑clustered water molecules may be absorbed more readily, promoting superior cellular hydration.
  • Improved hydration can support energy levels and aid recovery after intense activity.

Bone Health & Metabolic Support

Alkaline water contains minerals like calcium and magnesium, which contribute to maintaining strong bones. Preliminary studies suggest that alkaline water may improve bone mineral density in postmenopausal individuals when combined with calcium and vitamin D supplementation. Research also indicates that alkaline ionized water can help regulate blood glucose, cholesterol and triglyceride levels and protect pancreatic cells in animals.

  • Mineral content supports healthy bones and may complement osteoporosis therapy.
  • Ionized water improved metabolism and reduced blood glucose, cholesterol and triglycerides in laboratory studies.
  • Enhanced mineral uptake may also support muscle function and nerve conduction.

Antioxidant Power & Negative ORP

Oxidation–reduction potential (ORP) measures a fluid’s ability to act as an oxidant or antioxidant. Most tap water has a positive ORP of +200 to +400 mV, indicating that it is oxidising. In contrast, healthy internal environments are reductive (negative ORP). Water with a negative ORP may supply electrons that neutralise free radicals, acting as an antioxidant.

  • Negative ORP (about −350 mV) is similar to physiological fluids and helps counteract oxidative stress.
  • Alkaline water produced by electrolysis can have strong negative ORP, delivering antioxidant benefits without calories.
  • Our ionized water is engineered to provide optimal ORP levels while preserving essential minerals.

Digestive & Immune Support

Holy Elixir’s black water is infused with fulvic acid and 84 trace minerals. Fulvic acid contains electrolytes and amino acids that enhance nutrient absorption, support healthy digestion and help flush toxins from the body. Its antioxidant properties may bolster immune health by neutralising free radicals.

  • Rich mineral and amino acid content enhances nutrient uptake and gut health.
  • Antioxidant activity helps reduce oxidative stress and supports the immune system.
  • Fulvic acid aids detoxification and promotes healthy skin, hair and nails.

Safety & Balanced Perspective

While alkaline water may offer benefits, it’s important to maintain perspective. Major health authorities note that there is limited evidence to support claims that alkaline water can prevent disease or drastically alter body pH. Water with a pH above 9.8 can taste bitter and may lead to unwanted shifts in electrolyte levels, particularly in people with kidney disease.

  • There is little evidence that alkaline water prevents cancer, slows ageing or improves skin.
  • Excessively high pH (>9.8) or electrode degradation can cause irritation and rare side effects.
  • Our products are formulated within safe pH ranges and are intended to complement, not replace, a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle.
