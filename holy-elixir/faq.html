What is alkaline water?

Alkaline water is water with a pH greater than 7. Pure water has a neutral pH of 7, while tap water typically ranges from 6.5 to 8.5【246940208653990†L83-L96】【500576765799833†L102-L104】. Bottled alkaline water often has a pH between 8 and 9 and contains minerals such as calcium and magnesium carbonate【481118828845701†L174-L182】.

By increasing pH, alkaline water may temporarily buffer stomach acids and neutralise acidic foods; however, the body tightly regulates blood pH through kidney and lung function【500576765799833†L96-L113】.

Is alkaline water safe for everyone?

For most healthy adults, drinking water with a slightly higher pH is safe【865990079059878†L179-L190】. However, high‑pH water can taste bitter and, if consumed in excess (pH > 9.8), may raise blood electrolyte levels or interact with medications【865990079059878†L199-L203】【500576765799833†L119-L125】.

People with kidney disease or those taking proton pump inhibitors should consult their healthcare provider before regularly consuming alkaline water【500576765799833†L119-L125】. Holy Elixir products are formulated within safe pH ranges and are meant to complement a balanced diet.

What is ORP and why is it important?

ORP stands for oxidation–reduction potential, a measure of a fluid’s ability to donate or accept electrons. A positive ORP indicates oxidising water, while a negative ORP indicates reducing (antioxidant) water. Tap water typically has a positive ORP around +200 to +400 mV【492492353294600†L82-L119】, whereas Holy Elixir alkaline water has a negative ORP, supplying electrons that neutralise free radicals.

Healthy physiological fluids have negative ORP values【492492353294600†L82-L119】. Drinking water with a negative ORP can help counteract oxidative stress and support overall wellbeing.
