Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about alkaline water.

What is Alkaline Ionized Water?
Alkaline Ionized water is ionic water generated from a Water Ionizer through an electrolysis process. This results in water with an alkaline pH value ranging between 8.5 to 11, with no chemical additives.
How much should I drink daily?
Health experts recommend at least 8 to 10 glasses of water per day, or approximately 30 to 40 ml per kilogram of body weight.
Does alkaline water taste different?
Yes! Alkaline ionized water has a distinctively smooth, fresh taste with a subtle hint of "sweetness."
